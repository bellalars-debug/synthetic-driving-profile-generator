How this works. Your headcount seeds a synthetic workforce sampled from the 2022 U.S. National Household Travel Survey. Each synthetic driver gets a realistic commute, departure/return time, and daily trip chain; an income-linked EV share sets who drives electric. Their arrival profiles feed a station-and-queue charging simulation (Rongxin Yin's ev-infrastructure-tool). Chargers recommended is the peak number of vehicles charging at once on a design day, so no one waits. Home Level-2 drivers arrive well-charged; others top up at work — which is what creates the mid-morning peak.
